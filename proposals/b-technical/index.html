The loop

Every Sunday, your plan rewrites itself.

The first week comes from your goal race, your zones, and your schedule. Every week after that, it comes from what you actually did.

  1. 01

    Read

    Pull last 7 days from Garmin — HR, pace, vert, durations, RPE, sleep, HRV.

  2. 02

    Diagnose

    Compare against the plan. Detect drift, missed sessions, fatigue signals, and your free-text notes.

  3. 03

    Adapt

    Regenerate next week within periodization rules. Volume caps. Recovery weeks. One hard day at a time.

  4. 04

    Push

    Workouts land on your Garmin Connect calendar. Sync to watch is automatic. Open Garmin: it's there.

Origin

14 weeks of training. Then a product.

Switchback started as a personal tool for training for the Monte Rosa Walserwaeg Trail — 43 km, 3,200 m of vert, UTMB World Series, July 2026. Every workout type, every adaptation rule, every Garmin integration detail was tested on a real training block before it became a product.

— Peter Van Hee, founder
