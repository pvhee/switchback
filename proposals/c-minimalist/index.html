01 · The principle

A switchback lets you climb
terrain a straight line never could.

By zigzagging back and forth, each segment stays under the safe gradient. The total climb is steep. Each step is not. That's how trails get up mountains. It's also how training plans should work.

02 · How it works

Four steps.
Then the loop.

  1. 01 Describe your race in plain language.
  2. 02 Get a plan written for your zones and your schedule.
  3. 03 Workouts land on your Garmin Connect calendar.
  4. 04 Every Sunday, the plan rewrites itself from your data.

03 · For the steep end

Most plan apps stop at the marathon.
We start there.

  • Vertical-first workouts — hill repeats with vert targets, not just time.
  • Long days that look like the race — back-to-backs, hilly out-and-backs.
  • Effort-led where it matters — no HR alarm halfway up a climb.
  • AeT and LT drift checks keep your zones honest as you get fitter.
